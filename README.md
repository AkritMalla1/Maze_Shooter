## Maze Shooter

A fast-paced 3D first-person shooter game where tactical puzzle-solving meets bullet-hell combat.

### 🌌The Mission  
Trapped in a shifting labyrinth, you must navigate complex puzzles while fending off waves of aerial predators.  
Reach the heart of the arena to face the Final Boss and earn your freedom.

### 🚀 Key Features
**Dynamic 3D Combat:** Engage Red-fanged Vespera (Minions) that attack from all angles.  
**Brain-Teasing Puzzles:** Navigate through the maze and unlock the path forward.  
**Epic Boss Finale:** A multi-stage showdown against Scorp the Devourer (Final Boss) that tests everything you've learned.  
**Immersive Environment:** Illuminated Dark Fantasy.

### 🕹️ Gameplay & Objectives
**Explore:**	Navigate the 3D puzzle environment to find the exit.  
**Survive:**	Use your arsenal to take down flying enemies.  
**Conquer:**	Defeat the Final Boss to clear the level.  

### 🛠️ Controls
**WASD:** Movement  
**Mouse:** Aim & Shoot  
**Space:** Jump  
**Shift:** Sprint
**MouseScroll & 1234:** Toggle Weapons

### 📥 Installation
1. Download the [Maze Shooter_v1.exe](https://github.com/AkritMalla1/Maze_Shooter/blob/master/Maze%20Shooter_v1.exe)
2. Launch the file and Go Crazy
